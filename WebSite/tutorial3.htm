Creating a Character

The first time you login you will need to provide a name and password and select your character's gender. Your character is an Israelite, assisting King David in bringing peace and prosperity to the Kingdom. Your character name must reflect that. Male names can be found here. Female names can be found here. The character's gender has no effect on his/her strengths or weaknesses, but does have a bearing on how you role-play your character.
