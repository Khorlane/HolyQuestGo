June 15, 2022

Lost the history since 2003 :(. HolyQuest has been down for a while, but it is back. Enjoy!

November 7, 2003

Congratulations to Alex, first player to reach level 8!!! Alex wields the axe as his weapon of choice and recently acquired the silver needed to purchase a brand new axe. But, every time Alex tried to buy an axe from the shopkeeper, the shopkeeper would sell him a blunt axe instead. The shopkeeper has been spoken to concerning this matter and should now be more cooperative.

October 23, 2003

Travelers are reporting signs of thieves along the wilderness trail. The merchants of Jerusalem are complaining that this could hurt trade if left unchecked and are urging brave warriors to rid the land of this scourge.

October 14, 2003

A vineyard has been planted in the Kidron Valley. The owner has invited young warriors into the vineyard to help eliminate some pests.

October 3, 2003

The world of HolyQuest now has 200+ rooms for your adventurering pleasure! There is a great place for young warriors to hunt just south of Jerusalem. Directions: from Jesse Square, go west and out the city gates, go south as far as you can, go east to the Kindron Valley, go southwest to the Hinnom Valley, go west as far as you can, go northwest and you have arrived.

September 22, 2003

OMugs development continues. Currently there are 48 commands available. In game, use 'show commands' to get the currently implemented list of commands.

May 7, 2003

Complete website makeover is in progress.

March 7, 2003

HolyQuest is now running on the new mud server, OMugs - Online Multi-User Game Server. The world is still very small, but the basic mud commands are in place.

January 2, 2003

HolyQuest is still alive and well, although very few players and very small world. The mud server used to run HolyQuest is currently CircleMUD. But, about a year ago, work began on a new mud server named OMugs - Online Multi-User Game Server. OMugs is being coded from scratch using Microsoft Visual C++ and runs on a Microsoft Windows 2000 platform. The current plan is convert HolyQuest to run using OMugs sometimethis year and then continue the development of the HolyQuest world.

September 4, 2001

Today, in the early evening, a historic event occurred on HolyQuest. The first two players of HolyQuest, Ariel and Becca, logged in, found some equipment, and then some rats met their fate at the hands of these two fearless warriors.

There is more to the story, I won't tell all here, but there was some mysterious traveling and the statue incident! Ask them about it when; you see them 'in game' :)

Here's three cheers to Ariel and Becca, great warriors, who have joined David and his mighty men in their HolyQuest.
