Welcome to HolyQuest

HolyQuest is a game that allows many people to play at the same time via an internet connection. The game consists of a virtual world in which a player can participate in activities including, but not limited to, exploration, socializing, improving skills, buying, selling, and accumulating wealth.

The HolyQuest virtual world is actually a computer program plus many text files that 'tries' to simulate, real places, real people, and real things. But alas, it is only a computer program with limited abilities. You posses something that allows you to create a limitless world, your IMAGINATION. Your enjoyment of the game will be greatly enhanced when you use your imagination.
